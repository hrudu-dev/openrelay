About OpenRelay

OpenRelay is a modern, AI-powered, open-source technology media platform built to inform, educate, and connect tech enthusiasts, developers, startups, and professionals. With a community-first approach and open-access model, OpenRelay bridges the gap between real-time tech information and hands-on learning—all under one digital roof.

Mission

To democratize technology content and create an inclusive space for tech discovery, learning, and collaboration.

What Makes Us Unique

  • AI-powered insights: Summarized news, intelligent recommendations, and contextual content.
  • Open source: Built transparently, with community contributions.
  • Developer & learner focused: Real tools, tutorials, and reviews.
  • Community-driven: Forums and events for collaboration.

Open Collaboration

OpenRelay accepts contributions via GitHub, enabling developers, writers, and designers to co-create content, build tools, and shape the future of open-source tech media.
